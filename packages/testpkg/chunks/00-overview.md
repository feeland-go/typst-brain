## Overview

Touying (投影 in chinese, /tóuyǐng/, meaning projection) is a user-friendly, powerful and efficient package for creating presentation slides in Typst.

We maintain English and Chinese versions of the documentation for Touying, and for each major version, we maintain a documentation copy.

### Special Features
1. Split slides by headings
2. `#pause` and `#meanwhile` animations
3. Math Equation Animation
4. `touying-reducer` Cetz and Fletcher Animations
5. Correct outline and bookmark (no duplicate and correct page number)
6. Dewdrop Theme Navigation Bar
7. Semi-transparent cover mode
8. Speaker notes for dual-screen
9. Export slides to PPTX and HTML formats and show presentation online.
10. Instantly share touying slides on git.
