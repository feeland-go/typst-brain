# Exporting to PPTX and HTML

Touying slides can be exported to other formats using external tools.

### PPTX and HTML Export
Use [touying-exporter](https://github.com/touying-typ/touying-exporter) to convert your Typst slides to PowerPoint or HTML.

### Online Presentation
You can also show your presentation online using [touying-template](https://github.com/touying-typ/touying-template).
